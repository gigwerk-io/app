How To Request a FAVR

Swipe right to learn how to request your first task on FAVR

1. Select a Category

We have seven categories you can select from. Seasonal jobs like raking are currently our most popular.

2. Describe the Job

Describe the little intricacies of your job so your freelancer knows what they need to complete.

3. Set a Deadline

Select the day and time you want your job completed by.

4. Select a Location

Input the address of the job,

Note: we do not share your address until a freelancer is accepted.

5. Attach images (optional)

Attach photos as this greatly helps the freelancer know what they need to get done, though this is optional.

6. Select Difficulty

Select the difficulty of your job so freelancers know how tough the task at hand is.

7. Set Your Price

Lastly, how much are you willing to spend to have the task completed?

Note: jobs with an unreasonable price will likely receive no proposals and be ignored on the marketplace feed.
